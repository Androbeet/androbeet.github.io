## BUNDLE 01 — FEMINISM / PATRIARCHY

---

### Consortivulnus
*(consors = one who shares the same condition + vulnus = wound)*

The wound that comes specifically from someone who shares your oppression and uses it against you anyway. Not betrayal from an enemy. From someone inside the same cage.

---

### Virilnorm
*(virilis = masculine)*

The invisible standard all human achievement is measured against — including feminist achievement. "Strong woman" still means strong like a man.

---

### Gendarchy
*(gender + arche = rule)*

The governing structure that existed before law, survives law reform, and lives entirely in behavior. You can change constitutions. Gendarchy doesn't care.

---

### Matriveiling
*(mater = mother + velum = veil)*

The use of motherhood as a ceiling. Not a celebration of mothers. The reduction of a woman's entire existence to her reproductive capacity.

---

### Femivoria
*(femina + vorare = devour)*

The system's ability to consume feminist rebellion and sell it back as a product. Protest becomes merchandise. Revolution becomes an aesthetic.

---

## BUNDLE 02 — GOVERNMENT / SYSTEM

---

### Consensum Fictum
*(fictus = manufactured)*

Consent created by the absence of visible alternatives. You didn't agree. You just had nowhere else to go.

---

### Civitance
*(civitas = state)*

The specific numbness of living inside a system too large to individually resist. Not apathy. The rational response to structural scale.

---

### Nomocide
*(nomos = law + caedere = kill)*

The quiet death of a law through selective non-enforcement. The law exists on paper. The system decides whose paper it applies to.

---

### Clamocracy
*(clamor + kratos = rule)*

Governance by noise. The loudest position wins regardless of validity. Every modern democracy is partly a clamocracy.

---

### Democrasia
*(demos + krasis = dilution)*

The dilution of individual political will until it becomes statistically irrelevant. You voted. It didn't matter. The math ensured it from the beginning.

## BUNDLE 03 — PSYCHOLOGY

---

### Autopathia
*(auto + pathos)*

Knowing exactly what is wrong with you. Watching yourself do it anyway. The specific suffering of self-awareness that changes nothing.

---

### Proprialienation
*(proprius = own)*

Estrangement from your own emotions after years of suppressing them. You can describe what you feel. You cannot actually feel it anymore.

---

### Cogniveil

The comfortable false belief your mind maintains to protect you from a truth it cannot yet survive. The mind is not lying to you. It is managing you.

---

### Sanogenesis
*(sanus = healthy + genesis)*

The industry that profits from the process of healing but never from its completion. Your recovery is their business model.

---

### Egochronia
*(ego + chronos)*

The version of you that made a decision no longer exists by the time you are judged for it. Accountability assumes a continuous self. The self never stopped changing.

## BUNDLE 04 — PHILOSOPHY

---

### Veritaxis
*(veritas + taxis = arrangement)*

The unconscious arrangement of facts around a conclusion you already hold. You didn't find the truth. You constructed it backwards from your belief.

---

### Aporiaxis
*(aporia = no way through)*

The paralysis of someone who has thought deeply enough to see through every position. You cannot be fooled anymore. You also cannot move.

---

### Nihilocrux
*(nihil + crux = center)*

The central paradox of nihilism. If nothing matters — that statement also doesn't matter. Nihilism defeats itself in the same breath it speaks.

---

### Ontovoid
*(ontos = being)*

The philosophical vertigo of questioning your own existence from inside your own existence. The eye cannot examine itself.

---

### Reflexivore
*(reflexio + vorare = devour)*

Someone who consumes self-knowledge as a performance rather than a practice. They know every theory of the self. They have not changed once.

---

## BUNDLE 05 — SOCIETY / CULTURE

---

### Attentocide
*(attentio + caedere = kill)*

The systematic destruction of human attention span by the commercial internet. Not accidental. Attention is the product. Destroying it creates dependency.

---

### Visivore
*(visus = sight + vorare = devour)*

Someone who consumes content without processing it. The dominant mode of modern information interaction. Input without digestion.

---

### Culturvane
*(cultura + vane = weathervane)*

A belief that rotates with social wind rather than conviction. You don't hold the position. The position holds wherever the pressure is highest.

---

### Spectivism
*(spectare = to watch)*

The replacement of participation with observation. We don't act on injustice. We watch it. Then discuss watching it. Then watch discussions about the watching.

---

### Outragency
*(outrage + agency)*

The use of manufactured outrage as a substitute for actual power. You cannot change the system. But you can be very publicly angry about it. That anger is harvested and sold back as content.
