Bundle A

Interior States

Orvane
/or-VANE/
The feeling of being pulled toward somewhere you have never been, with no rational reason attached. Not wanderlust. A directionless pull — a gravity with no named source.
Selumin
/sel-OO-min/
The warmth of realizing someone remembered a small detail about you that you mentioned once and never repeated. The specific evidence that someone was actually listening.
Morven
/MOR-ven/
The quiet ache of loving someone who is completely fine without you. Not jealousy. Not bitterness. The weight of being unnecessary to someone you needed.
Arven
/AR-ven/
The brief, unexplained certainty of being exactly where you are supposed to be. No logic. No cause. Just a quiet conviction that arrives without permission and leaves the same way.
Calveth
/KAL-veth/
The sensation of watching your own life from a slight emotional remove — present in it, but observing it simultaneously. Not dissociation. Something quieter and more functional.
Athelm
/ATH-elm/
The settled state of carrying two completely contradictory beliefs simultaneously — not as confusion, but as something you have organized your life around. A functioning internal contradiction.
Selmir
/SEL-mir/
The instinct to make yourself smaller in a new room before anyone has given you a reason to. Preemptive reduction. The self-diminishment that arrives before the threat does.
Draveth
/DRAV-eth/
The state of functioning at full external capacity while internally at zero. The performance of continuity when the interior has gone quiet and you're not sure when it will return.
Silven
/SIL-ven/
The recurring curiosity about the version of yourself that would have existed under different circumstances. Not regret for choices made. A quiet awareness of unchosen parallel selves.
Bundle B

The Body

Serath
/SER-ath/
The physical sensation in the sternum when music matches your exact emotional state. Not emotion produced by music — the chest's response to being accurately described by sound.
Calvine
/KAL-vine/
The sensation of your face returning to its resting expression the moment you leave a social situation. The muscles releasing. The expression nobody else gets to see.
Lacren
/LAK-ren/
The specific quality of your eyes after crying — the slight heaviness, the altered light. The world looking marginally different for hours after. Like the atmosphere changed.
Orethic
/or-ETH-ik/
The involuntary stillness the body enters upon hearing something that is precisely true. Not shock. Not surprise. The physical response to exact accuracy.
Bundle C

Thought & Language

Voreth
/VOR-eth/
The specific paralysis produced by having too many options. Not indecision. The freeze that scale creates — where every beginning cancels out every other beginning.
Drevin
/DREH-vin/
Understanding exactly what someone meant even though what they said was technically something different. The comprehension that operates underneath language.
Sulith
/SOO-lith/
The thought that arrives the moment a conversation ends — the precise thing you should have said, in the exact form it should have been said. It only comes when it is too late.
Bundle D

Between People

Ostren
/OS-tren/
The specific closeness between two people who communicate better through what they don't say than through what they do. A bond built in the negative space of language.
Verlan
/VER-lan/
The particular intimacy that forms between two people who have witnessed each other at their absolute lowest. Not love necessarily. A bond made of evidence rather than time.
Corvane
/kor-VANE/
The desire not to be widely known, but to be completely known by one person. The hunger for depth of witness over breadth of audience.
Veran
/VEH-ran/
The desire for a conversation you cannot yet have because the person capable of having it with you doesn't exist in your life yet.
Solith
/SOL-ith/
The longing to be witnessed in an ordinary moment — not an impressive one. To be seen doing nothing remarkable and have that be enough.
Droven
/DROH-ven/
The moment in a relationship when both people realize simultaneously, without speaking, that something has changed permanently. The mutual recognition that lands before either person finds the words.
Alveran
/al-VEH-ran/
The specific care one person takes to ensure the other never knows how much they are carrying alone. The protection made of silence. Love expressed as concealment.
Orvel
/OR-vel/
The mutual, unspoken agreement in a room to move past an uncomfortable moment without acknowledging it. The social contract operating at its most basic and most essential.
Bundle E

System

Sulvarch
/SUL-varch/
The structural expectation that certain people will absorb institutional discomfort, awkwardness, and emotional weight on behalf of everyone around them — without acknowledgment, as a baseline condition of their presence.
Corveth
/KOR-veth/
The process by which power rebrands its own cruelty as policy, procedure, or necessity. The language system that makes oppression sound administrative.
Delvane
/DEL-vane/
The deliberate cultivation of public helplessness — a system engineering the belief that resistance is impossible before resistance is even attempted.
